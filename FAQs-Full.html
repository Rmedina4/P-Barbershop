PARADISE BARBERSHOP - FAQs

FIND THE ANSWERS FOR THE MOST FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

DO I NEED AN APPOINTMENT?

NOT AT ALL! ANYONE IS WELCOME TO WALK IN AND BE ATTENDED DURING BUSNIESS HOURS!

SO HOW CAN I BOOK MY APPOINTMENT?

EASY! BOOK YOUR APPOINTMENT WITH ANY OF OUR BARBERS DIRECTLY THROUGH OUR WEBSITE. TYPE YOUR NAME AND NUMBER, FOR A TEXT CONFIRMATION.

HOW EARLY CAN I BOOK?

CURRENTLY, YOU CAN BOOK 7 DAYS IN ADVANCE. PLEASE CANCEL 24 HOURS PRIOR TO GIVE THE BARBER A HEADS UP!

WHAT PAYMENT METHOD IS ACCEPTED?

CASH, APPLE PAY, VISA, MASTERCARD, AMEX, DISCOVER, ETC...

WHERE CAN I PARK?

WE ARE FACING FERGUSON RD. WHERE PARKING IS LIMITED. RIGHT AROUND THE CORNER THERE ARE A TON OF PARKING SPOTS THAT ARE A ONE MINUTE WALK FROM THE SHOP.

WHAT ARE THE SHOP HOURS?

CURRENTLY WE ARE OPEN EVERYDAY FROM 10 AM - 7 PM.

NOTE: HOURS MAY VARY DURING HOLIDAYS. PLEASE CONNECT WITH US ON SOCIAL MEDIA TO KEEP UP WITH ALL LATEST HOURS ,NEWS, PROMOTIONS, ETC...
